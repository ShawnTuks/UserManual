Hire Applicant  

This functionality describes the process of a HR manager hiring an applicant/s. The HR manager will navigate to the job cards screen and select to view the job card they wish to hire an applicant for. They will select the interviews tab and select the applicant on their interview slot that has been conducted. The HR manager will then select the hire button and the applicant will be notified that they have been successful.
