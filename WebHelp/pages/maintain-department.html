Maintain Department  

This functionality describes the process whereby the systems admin either updates or remove a department from the system. The Systems Admin Navigates to the company screen. The system will display the list of departments in the System.  

The Systems Admin will choose to: Update a department by clicking on the pencil button on the department, which will lead them to enter the updated department which will then be updated in the system or Delete the department by clicking on the trash can button which will lead to the department being removed from the system.
