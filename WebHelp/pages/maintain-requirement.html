Maintain Requirement  

This functionality describes the process whereby the systems admin either updates or remove a requirement from the system. The Systems Admin Navigates to the skills, requirements, and questions screen. The system will display the list of requirements in the System.  

The Systems Admin will choose to: Update a skill by clicking on the pencil button on the requirement, which will lead them to enter the updated requirement which will then be updated in the system or Delete the requirement by clicking on the trash can button which will lead to the requirement being removed from the system.
