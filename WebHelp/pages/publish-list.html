Publish List  

This functionality describes the process of an HR Manager publishing the shortlist and notifying the disqualified applicants that they have not been successful. The HR manager will navigate to their Hire Manager Control and select the unconfirmed shortlist they want to approve. Once the HR manager is satisfied with the shortlist, they will select to disqualify non shortlisted, and the unsuccessful applicants will be notified.
