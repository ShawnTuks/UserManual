Create Shortlist 

This functionality describes the process of a recruiter creating the shortlist. The recruiter will navigate to the job card they wish to create the shortlist for and request to view the applicant pool. The system will then sort all applicants to their columns according to the applicant's status of shortlist, disqualified or undecided. The recruiter will then sort through all applicants and put the applicant in either the disqualified or shortlist column. Once all applicants have been sorted and the shortlist is complete. The recruiter will then confirm the list and the HR manager will then be notified.
