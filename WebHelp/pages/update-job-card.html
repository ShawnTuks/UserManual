Update Job Card  

This functionality describes the process of a recruiter updating the information of a job card that they have previously created. A recruiter will only be allowed to update a job card when it is in the approving or approved stage. The recruiter will navigate to their job cards and select to view the job card they wish to update. The recruiter will then select to edit the job card and will update the information they wish and save it to the system.
