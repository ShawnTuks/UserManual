Remove Team Member  

This functionality describes the process whereby the Systems administrator removes a team member. The systems administrator will navigate to the teams’ screen. The screen displays the list of teams. The systems admin will click on the three dots on the team name for more options. This will display the team details as well as the members in the team. The systems admin will click on the x button next to the members’ name. This will remove the team member from the team
